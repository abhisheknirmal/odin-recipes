Lemon-Blueberry Cupcakes with Blueberry-Lemon Buttercream Frosting

Zesty and bright, these cupcakes are sure to wow! A vibrant frosting (flavored with a quick homemade jam) sits atop a
lemony, moist cupcake studded with colorful berries. Using frozen blueberries makes these cupcakes accessible any
time of year, too!

Ingredients

Blueberry Jam

  • 1.5 cups frozen blueberries
  • 2 tablespoon white sugar
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice

Cupcakes

  • 1 ¾ cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup white sugar
